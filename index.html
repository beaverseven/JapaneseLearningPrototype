JLPT N5

Description

After this course:
You will be able to read basic sentences in hiragana and katakana.
You will be able to read your first 100 kanji.
You will be able to conversate about daily life topics in a basic manner.

JLPT N4

Description

After this course:
You will be able to further understand daily life topics spoken in a slow manner.
You will be able to read up to about 300 kanji.
You will be able to further conversate about daily life topics and express yourself a bit more freely.
