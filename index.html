JLPT N5

What is N5?

N5 is the first step for any learner to test their knowledge of Japanese. It is the lowest level in the JLPT (日本語能力試験) ranging from 5 (lowest) to 1 (highest). This level consists of the building blocks of the language that will prepare you to formulate sentences that will expand at higher levels

After this course, you will be able to:

  • You will be able to read basic sentences in hiragana and katakana.
  • You will be able to read your first 100 kanji.
  • You will be able to conversate about daily life topics in a basic manner.

JLPT N4

What is N4?

N4 is a level of difficulty for the Japanese Language Proficiency Exam

After this course, you will be able to:

  • You will be able to further understand daily life topics spoken in a slow manner.
  • You will be able to read up to about 300 kanji.
  • You will be able to further conversate about daily life topics and express yourself a bit more freely.
